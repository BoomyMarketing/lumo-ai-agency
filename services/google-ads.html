What We Manage

Google Ads Management Built for Performance, Not Vanity Metrics

Most agencies optimize Google Ads for clicks and impressions. Lumo optimizes for revenue. Every campaign we manage is structured around conversion data, ROAS targets, and the economic unit metrics that determine whether your paid media is actually growing your business.

We operate across Search, Performance Max, Shopping, Display, and YouTube — building campaign architectures that cover the full customer journey from awareness to purchase. Our account structures use tightly themed ad groups, single-keyword-per-intent strategies for high-value terms, and smart bidding algorithms calibrated to your specific margin and volume targets.

Our AI-assisted creative workflow generates and tests hundreds of headline and description combinations for Responsive Search Ads, continuously surfacing the messaging that drives the highest CTR and conversion rate in your specific market. Nothing runs on gut feel — everything is decided by data.

Conversion tracking is non-negotiable at Lumo. We implement server-side tracking, Enhanced Conversions, and GA4 integrations that give Google's algorithms the cleanest possible signal — and give you accurate attribution for every lead or sale. Bad data is the number-one reason Google Ads underperforms.

Common Questions

Google Ads FAQ

What types of Google Ads campaigns does Lumo manage?

We manage Search, Performance Max, Shopping, Display, and YouTube campaigns. For most clients we recommend a multi-campaign strategy that covers each stage of the funnel — from awareness through YouTube and Display to high-intent conversion via Search. We always build campaigns around your specific business objectives and ROAS targets.

How does Lumo use AI in Google Ads management?

We leverage Google's Smart Bidding algorithms layered with our own performance intelligence — monitoring auction insights, search term patterns, and Quality Score trends. We use AI-assisted creative generation for Responsive Search Ads, automated A/B testing, and predictive budget allocation to maximize ROAS across shifting demand patterns.

What ROAS can I expect from Google Ads?

ROAS varies by industry, competition, and product margin. Our clients average 4–8x ROAS across e-commerce verticals and $30–$80 CPL for B2B lead generation campaigns. We set realistic benchmarks based on your industry data in our first strategy session and optimize toward them monthly.

Do you require a minimum ad spend?

We recommend a minimum ad spend of $3,000/mo to generate statistically significant data for optimization. For e-commerce or competitive B2B categories, $5,000–$10,000/mo is more appropriate. Our management fee is separate from ad spend and paid directly to Lumo — ad spend goes directly to Google.

How quickly can you launch a new Google Ads campaign?

New campaign builds typically launch within 7–14 business days. This includes keyword research, ad group structure, creative development, landing page review, conversion tracking setup, and audience configuration. Existing account takeovers can often be optimized within 48 hours of access being granted.
