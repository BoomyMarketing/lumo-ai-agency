Why It Matters

Why Your Google Business Profile Is Your Most Important Local Asset

When someone searches for your service locally, the Google local pack (the map with 3 business listings) appears above all organic results and often above paid ads. The businesses in the local pack capture the majority of local search clicks — and GBP optimisation is the primary factor determining who appears there. A complete, well-managed GBP profile with strong review signals outranks incomplete competitors regardless of website quality.

Most businesses set up their GBP once and never touch it again. This is a significant competitive opportunity. Google actively rewards GBP profiles that are kept current — regular posts, photo uploads, updated business hours, answered questions, and active review responses all signal to Google that your business is active and engaged. Inactive profiles lose local pack position to competitors who are actively managing theirs.

Lumo's GBP management program covers every signal that influences local pack ranking: profile completeness, category selection, business description optimisation, photo strategy, regular Google Post publishing, Q&A management, and review generation and response. We monitor your local pack position weekly and adjust our approach based on what's driving ranking movement in your specific market.
