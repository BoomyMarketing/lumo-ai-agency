Common Questions

Content Marketing FAQ

How does Lumo use AI in content production?

We use LLMs for research acceleration, keyword integration mapping, first-draft generation, and optimization suggestions. All content is reviewed, substantially edited, and fact-checked by human SEO writers before publishing. We use AI to go faster — not to replace quality.

How long does it take for content to rank?

New content typically begins ranking within 4-8 weeks on lower-competition queries. Competitive head terms can take 3-6 months to reach page 1. We prioritize a mix of quick-win lower-competition terms and strategic longer-term target keywords to show ROI early while building toward bigger rankings.

Do you write about technical topics?

Yes. We specialize in technical and B2B content marketing — AI, SaaS, fintech, marketing technology, and other complex verticals. We work with subject matter experts on your team to ensure accuracy on highly technical topics, and our writers have domain expertise across multiple industries.

What is a content pillar page and do I need one?

A pillar page is a comprehensive, long-form resource that covers a broad topic (e.g., "AI Marketing") and links to cluster content covering subtopics. This structure signals topical authority to search engines and typically ranks faster for competitive terms. We recommend pillar pages for any topic cluster where you want to build sustained organic authority.

Can you refresh existing content that has dropped in rankings?

Yes. Content refreshes are often the highest-ROI activity in a content program. We audit your existing content, identify pages that have lost rankings or traffic, and update them with current information, improved structure, better keyword targeting, and enhanced E-E-A-T signals to recover and improve rankings.
