What We Do

Paid Media Strategy Built Around Your Unit Economics

Most agencies optimize PPC for clicks. Lumo optimizes for revenue. That distinction determines whether paid media is a growth lever or a budget drain for your business. We start every PPC engagement by understanding your unit economics — LTV, gross margin, CAC targets — and building a paid media strategy designed to hit those numbers.

Our PPC practice covers the full paid media landscape: Google Search and Performance Max for high-intent capture, Microsoft Ads for incremental reach at lower CPCs, LinkedIn for B2B audience targeting with job title and company precision, and programmatic display for brand building and retargeting. We allocate budget to the channels where your specific buyers are active at the right stage of their journey.

AI-assisted optimization runs across every account we manage. Smart bidding algorithms calibrated to your revenue data, automated creative testing that surfaces winners faster, anomaly detection that catches spend waste before it compounds, and predictive budget allocation that shifts spend toward opportunities in real time.

Attribution is where most PPC management falls short. We implement multi-touch attribution models, server-side conversion tracking, and cross-channel reporting that shows you where revenue is actually coming from — not just which last-click source gets the credit. Better attribution data leads to better allocation decisions and higher overall ROAS.

Common Questions

PPC Management FAQ

What PPC channels does Lumo manage?

We manage Google Ads (Search, Performance Max, Shopping, Display, YouTube), Microsoft Ads, LinkedIn Ads, Meta Ads (Facebook and Instagram), and programmatic display through demand-side platforms. We recommend channel mix based on your audience, budget, and business model — not on where we make the highest margins.

How does Lumo use AI in PPC management?

We use AI at multiple layers: Google and Microsoft Smart Bidding for real-time bid optimization, AI-assisted creative generation for RSAs and display ads, anomaly detection for spend waste identification, and predictive budget allocation models that shift spend toward opportunities as auction dynamics change.

What attribution model do you use?

We implement multi-touch attribution with a preference for data-driven attribution where volume allows. We also implement server-side conversion tracking (Enhanced Conversions for Google, Conversions API for Meta) to recover attribution lost to ad blockers and iOS restrictions. Better data equals better decisions.

Do you manage ad creative or just bidding?

We manage the full scope of paid media — not just bidding. This includes creative strategy, copy testing, landing page consultation, audience development, and creative refresh recommendations. Creative is the biggest variable in paid media performance, and we treat it as seriously as the technical optimization layer.

What minimum ad spend do you require?

We recommend a minimum of $5,000/mo in combined ad spend across channels to generate sufficient data for optimization and deliver meaningful results. For single-channel campaigns targeting niche B2B audiences on LinkedIn, a minimum of $3,000/mo is appropriate. Management fees are separate and paid directly to Lumo.
