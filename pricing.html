Common Questions

Pricing FAQ

What's included in the onboarding process?

All plans start with a full onboarding sprint — we audit your existing stack, set up tracking and dashboards, map out your 90-day roadmap, and kick off the first deliverables within 7 days of signing.

Can I upgrade or change my plan mid-month?

Yes. You can upgrade at any time — we'll prorate the difference. Downgrades take effect on the next billing cycle. There are no penalties for changing plans.

Do the plans include ad spend?

No — plan fees cover management, strategy, and execution. Ad spend (Google, Meta, etc.) is billed directly to your ad account and is separate from Lumo's management fee. We recommend starting with at least $1,500/mo in ad spend on the Starter plan.

What if I only need one or two services?

We offer standalone service engagements. Contact us for a custom quote on individual services like AI automation builds, web design projects, or a single ad channel managed. Project-based work is priced separately.

How long before I see results?

AI automations and paid ads typically show measurable impact within 30 days. SEO and content take 60–90 days to build momentum. We set clear KPI baselines in week one so you always know what's being measured and when to expect movement.
