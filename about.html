How We Started

Built by Engineers
Obsessed With AI

Lumo was founded in Austin, TX by a group of engineers and performance marketers who saw the same pattern everywhere: agencies promising AI-powered results while running the same manual campaigns they had in 2015.

We took a different approach. Every service we offer is built on a foundation of machine learning, automation, and data infrastructure — not buzzwords. From AI-powered outbound sequences to custom LLM workflows, Lumo delivers compounding, measurable growth.

Today, we work with over 150 businesses ranging from seed-stage startups to $50M revenue companies, helping them deploy AI as a genuine competitive advantage — not a marketing add-on.
