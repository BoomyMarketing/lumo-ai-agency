Why It Matters

Why Pet Businesses Need Specialist Digital Marketing

Pet ownership is at an all-time high in the US — over 66% of households own a pet, and pet owners spend more on veterinary and grooming services than ever before. When a new pet owner moves to the area or a current pet owner looks for a specialist, the first step is almost always Google. The practices and businesses that appear prominently in local search win those clients.

Veterinary and pet service marketing has unique considerations: healthcare compliance for vet practices, the emotional attachment of pet owners to their animals, and intensely local search intent for grooming, boarding, and daycare services. Lumo builds marketing strategies calibrated to the specific business model and client psychology of each pet industry segment.

AI-powered search is also changing pet service discovery. When someone asks ChatGPT 'best vet near me for large dogs' or 'dog boarding with webcams in Seattle,' the businesses cited win high-value clients. Lumo integrates GEO optimisation to ensure your pet business is included in AI-generated recommendations.
