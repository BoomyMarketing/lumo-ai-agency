Why Is Your Current Digital Marketing Not Growing?

Stagnant digital marketing programs almost universally share three root causes. First: insufficient content volume. Google's algorithm rewards websites that demonstrate deep, consistent expertise across a topic area. Publishing 4-8 articles per month cannot establish topical authority — a competitor publishing 40+ will outrank you even with lower domain authority, because Google perceives them as the more comprehensive resource.

Second: misaligned keyword targeting. Many businesses invest in content that targets keywords with high competition and low conversion intent, while ignoring the long-tail, buyer-intent queries where ranking is achievable and conversion rates are 3-5x higher. Lumo's AI keyword engine analyzes intent signals in search queries — not just search volume — to identify the terms that actually convert to paying customers in your specific market.

Third: technical debt. Slow page load times, crawl errors, incorrect canonicalization, and missing schema markup collectively suppress rankings even for sites with excellent content. Lumo's automated weekly technical audit catches these issues before they compound, ensuring your content investment is not being undermined by fixable infrastructure problems.
